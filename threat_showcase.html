About "Threat"

"Threat" is an intense, fast-paced arcade-style game where players must navigate treacherous environments, defuse bombs, and outwit enemies under extreme time pressure. Developed with a retro aesthetic, it aims to evoke the thrilling challenge of classic 8-bit and 16-bit games.

Key Features: Dynamic levels, challenging puzzles, pixel-perfect collision, and a high-energy chiptune soundtrack. Prepare for a blast!
